About Us

We specialize in organizing entertainment events, with over 20 years of experience in the field. As one of the pioneering companies in Saudi Arabia, we had the honor of hosting the first-ever musical concerts in the Kingdom.

Our company has also played a major role in organizing various championship celebrations across major sports events such as The Abdul Latif Jameel League, Roshn Saudi League and The King's Cup.

We were also proud to organize the "Ittihad 90" celebration, marking 90 years since the founding of Al-Ittihad Club.
